BIT CULTURAL SOCIETY

"Weaving threads to our culture"

BCS, BIT Cultural Society is an initiative by Professor Ajay Oroan along with some students of BIT Sindri for reinforcing and uplifting the lesser privileged, as well as escorting and pursuing the motto of welfare for individuals of this institute in every term and aspects. It also showcases the rich traditional culture and art of the indigenous tribes that are found in Jharkhand.


HO

An austroasiatic speaking ethnic group of India. They are mostly concentrated in the state of Jharkhand. They mainly speak Ho.

SANTHAL

An ethnic group native to India and the largest tribe in Jharkhand. The santhals mostly speak Santhali.

MUNDA

Munda people are an ethnic group of India. They speak the Mundari as their native language which belongs to the Munda subgroup of Austroasiatic languages.

ORAON

Oraon is another ethnic group which mainly resides in Jharkhand. They mainly speaks two languages, Oraon and Kurukh.

SARHUL PUJA

Every year, BCS catalouges Sarhul Puja in new calendar month. Sarhul is dedicated to elements of nature and festival starts with the worship of trees.
Sarhul Festival organized by BCS, comprises of many further programmes including Quiz surrounding around Lifestyle of Jharkhand, Essay Writing and many more cultural events.

KARMA

BCS organises Karma Puja in the month of September.
Karam or Karma is a harvest festival celebrated in Indian state of Jharkhand Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Assam, Odisha and West Bengal. It is dedicated to the worship of Karam-Devta, the god of power, youth, and youthfulness.

KARMA FEST

After the tedious work and exertion in series, everyone needs a smash to freshen up, to firm up, to gear up again with full exuberance. It is basically a cultural event that carries all the magic of Art and Music in order to prompt our culture.
